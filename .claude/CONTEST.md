# CONTEST.md - Blackwell NVFP4 Kernel Hackathon

## Overview
- **Partners:** NVIDIA, GPU MODE, Dell, and Sesterce.
- **Objective:** Optimize four specific kernels (Batched GEMV, GEMM, Gated Dual GEMM, and Grouped GEMM).
- **Target Precision:** NVFP4 (NVIDIA 4-bit Floating Point).

## Submission & Eligibility
- **Registration:** Must be registered at [luma.com/9n27uem4](https://luma.com/9n27uem4).
- **Submission Channel:** Discord `#submissions` (GPU MODE Server).
- **Evaluation:** Speed-of-light performance closest to hardware roofline.
- **Ethics:** Only open-source code; no obfuscation.

## Challenge Problems (Focus Area)
1. **Problem 2 (GEMM):** Our current focus. Must beat the published Blackwell baseline.
2. **Key Metrics:** Arithmetic Intensity (FLOPs/byte) and absolute latency.

## Prizes (The "Workstation" Goal)
- **Grand Prize:** Dell Pro Max Workstation with GB300 (Blackwell Ultra).
- **1st Place:** NVIDIA DGX Spark + GTC 2026 Pass.
- **2nd Place:** NVIDIA RTX 5090 + GTC 2026 Pass.
- **3rd Place:** NVIDIA RTX 5080.
